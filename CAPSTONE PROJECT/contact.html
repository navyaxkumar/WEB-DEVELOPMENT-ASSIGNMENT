Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery through Swiggy and Zomato. You can also call us directly for delivery orders.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we have valet parking available. Paid parking is also available in Connaught Place.

Do you cater for private events?

Yes, we offer catering services for private events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us for details.

Are reservations mandatory?

Reservations are not mandatory but highly recommended, especially during peak hours and weekends.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we have an extensive vegetarian menu with authentic North Indian vegetarian dishes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets.
