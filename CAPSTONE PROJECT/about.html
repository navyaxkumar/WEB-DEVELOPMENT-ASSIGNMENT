About Us

Our Story, Our Passion, Our Commitment

Welcome to Swaad Restaurant

Established in 2010, Swaad Restaurant has been serving authentic North Indian cuisine to the heart of Delhi for over a decade. Our name "Swaad" means "taste" in Hindi, and that's exactly what we promise - an unforgettable taste experience that transports you to the vibrant streets and royal kitchens of North India.

Our Story

Founded by Chef Rajesh Kumar, who hails from a family of traditional cooks in Punjab, Swaad Restaurant was born from a simple dream: to share the authentic flavors of North Indian home cooking with food lovers across Delhi.

What started as a small 20-seater restaurant has now grown into one of Delhi's most beloved dining destinations, known for our commitment to quality, authenticity, and hospitality.

Our Philosophy

At Swaad, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. We source the finest spices from across India, use fresh vegetables from local markets, and prepare everything in-house using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our chefs take pride in slow-cooking our signature dishes, allowing the spices to blend perfectly and create those deep, complex flavors that North Indian cuisine is famous for.
